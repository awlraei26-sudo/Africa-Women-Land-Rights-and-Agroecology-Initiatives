ABOUT AWLRAEI

Empowering Women Through Land Rights, Agroecology And Climate Action

Africa Women Land Rights and Agro Ecology Initiatives (AWLRAEI) is committed to empowering women and communities by promoting equitable land rights, sustainable agriculture, climate resilience, environmental conservation and inclusive development.

We believe that when women have secure land rights, leadership opportunities and access to sustainable agricultural knowledge, communities become more prosperous, resilient and food secure.

Read Our Vision
WHO WE ARE

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A just and sustainable Africa where women have secure land rights, practice resilient agroecology and lead thriving, inclusive communities free from poverty, inequality and environmental degradation.

Our Mission

To empower women and communities across Africa through secure land rights, agroecological farming, climate resilience, gender equality, leadership and sustainable development.

OUR VALUES

Core Values

Gender Equality

Promoting equal rights, opportunities and participation for women and girls.

Justice & Equity

Advocating for fair access to land, resources and sustainable livelihoods.

Sustainability

Protecting natural resources through environmentally responsible practices.

Empowerment

Building leadership, knowledge and economic capacity among women.

Collaboration

Working with governments, communities, civil society and development partners.

Innovation

Encouraging creative local solutions for agriculture and environmental challenges.
