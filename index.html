Our Mission

To empower women and communities across Africa by advancing secure land rights, promoting agroecological farming, strengthening climate resilience, and building the capacity of women to lead in agriculture, conservation, and policy.

Why AWLRAEI exists

Across much of Africa, women grow the food but rarely hold title to the land they farm. That gap keeps households poorer, soils more degraded, and communities more exposed to climate shocks than they need to be. AWLRAEI works at the intersection of land justice and regenerative farming, because neither can hold without the other.
